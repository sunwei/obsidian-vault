# Core Advantages

1. **Seamless Integration with Obsidian**
    
    Publish your notes directly from Obsidian with minimal steps. No exports, no extra tools needed.
    
2. **One-Click Publishing**
    
    Simply right-click any folder or note, and your content is live online immediately.
    
3. **Beautiful & Customizable Templates**
    
    Choose from official or community templates, or customize CSS/JS to create a website that matches your style.
    
4. **WYSIWYG Local Preview**
    
    See exactly how your website looks before publishing, ensuring layout and style are perfect.
    
5. **Full Data Privacy & Ownership**
    
    All data remains under your control. Friday does not store or access your content unnecessarily.
    
6. **Multi-Platform Deployment**
    
    Supports Netlify, GitHub Pages, Vercel, or private deployment via FTP/SCP, giving flexibility and control.
    
7. **Cost-Effective**
    
    Free basic plan allows anyone to get started, with optional premium services for advanced features.
    
8. **Time-Saving & Beginner-Friendly**
    
    No coding, no server setup, no complex configurations. Focus on creating content; Friday handles the rest.
